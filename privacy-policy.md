Privacy Policy

This application uses the device camera to allow users to capture images within the app.
Images are processed only for the intended app functionality.

The app does not collect, store, or share personal data with third parties.
Captured images are not transmitted to any external servers unless explicitly initiated by the user.

The app does not use the camera in the background.
Camera access is requested only when required for core features.
